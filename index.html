Simple Homemade Bread

Bake delicious bread effortlessly with this simple recipe. Perfect for beginners!

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour – 500g, sifted.
  • Water – 350ml, lukewarm.
  • Active dry yeast – 7g (one sachet).
  • Salt – 10g (about 1.5 tsp).
  • Sugar – a pinch.
  • Olive oil – a drizzle.
  • Optional: Herbs (rosemary, thyme) – for added flavor.
